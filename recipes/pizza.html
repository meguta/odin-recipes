Pizza

Description

This recipe will produce a ciruclar, and geometrical and tasty pizza. This is one of the most common dishes to have with friends and family, and despite popular beliefs, is very easy to make. Most people will have the basic ingredients in order to make this masterpiece of a recipe.

Ingredients

  • Flour - 3 cups
  • Cooking Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Yeast - 2 teaspoons
  • Salt - 2 tablespoons
  • Water - 1.5 cups
  • Tomato Sauce - 1 cup
  • Shredded Mozzarela - 1 cup

Steps

  1. Create the dough
    In a big bowl, combine the water, cooking oil, and yeast into a mixture. Slowly add the flour until the mixture resembles a dough-like substance and consistency. After that, equally divide into 4 parts, and store overnight.

  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees farheiheit
    Preheat the oven and proceed to take out dough from fridge overnight.

  3. Assemble the pizza
    Spread out the dough in the shape you want your pizza. Spread the tomato sauce, then the mozzarela over the dough.

  4. Cook the pizza
    Put the pizza into the oven for 10-12 minutes, or until crust is golden brown

  5. Take out pizza
    Take your finished pizza, cut into 8-10 slices, and enjoy!
